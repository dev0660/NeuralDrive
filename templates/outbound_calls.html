Welcome to Outbound Calls

📞 Enhance Your Selling Skills with AI-Powered Training Calls

Our AI Voice Bots simulate realistic customer interactions, each with unique personalities, objections, and buying scenarios, to sharpen your sales abilities and boost your confidence.

🚀 How to Use:

  • Answer Naturally: Treat every call as if you're speaking with a real customer.
  • Handle Objections: Listen carefully, respond thoughtfully, and practice overcoming common buying hesitations.
  • Review Feedback: After each call, review the detailed evaluation provided in your dashboard to pinpoint strengths and areas for improvement.

📈 Get Better Every Call:

  • Gain Practical Experience: Engage regularly to master different customer scenarios.
  • Learn and Adjust: Leverage personalized insights and call transcripts to refine your approach.
  • Track Your Progress: Monitor your ongoing improvement through performance metrics and success evaluations.

🎯 Visual Progress Indicators:

Each user’s button reflects your progress. Buttons start as blue until you successfully book an appointment with that user. Once booked, the button color updates based on your performance score:

  • 100: Gold (Perfect score)
  • 90–99: Excellent
  • 75–89: Strong
  • 60–74: Solid
  • 45–59: Needs Improvement
  • 30–44: Weak
  • 15–29: Poor
  • 0–14: Critical

*Note: Highest scores are saved for each VoiceBot.
