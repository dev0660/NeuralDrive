Features 🚀

📞 Realistic AI Sales Simulations

Train salespeople with ultra-realistic, voice-based outbound call simulations that mimic real dealership sales scenarios.

🔁 Scenario Customization

We can build and tweak AI customer personas and scenarios to fit any sales process, product line, or dealership campaign.

📊 Automatic Sales Rep Evaluation

Each call is analyzed instantly with AI-driven feedback, giving reps detailed scores and coaching on objection handling, tone, product knowledge, and more.

📈 Performance Analytics Dashboard (Currently In the Works)

Managers get full visibility into rep performance with real-time dashboards, historical comparisons, and exportable reports.

🧠 AI Objection Library

Continuously expanding database of real-world objections (e.g. “I need to talk to my wife first”) that AI customers can throw at sales reps — ensuring reps are always prepared for the most common and toughest objections.

🕒 Call Scheduling & Reminders (Currently In the Works)

Allow reps to schedule practice calls and receive reminders, creating consistent daily training habits without manual oversight.

🎯 Personalized Training Plans (Currently In the Works)

Automatically generate improvement plans based on a rep’s performance history — e.g. "Work on handling price objections" or "Improve call openers".

🎥 Post-Call Replay & Annotations (Currently In the Works)

Reps and managers can review recordings with AI-generated annotations showing key moments, missed opportunities, and strengths.

🔄 Multi-Scenario Rotation

Rotate between different call scenarios: lease vs. finance, first-time buyers, trade-in discussions, cold leads, etc. — so reps don’t get too used to one script.

🔐 Data Security & Compliance

We follow industry best practices to keep your call data secure and confidential.
