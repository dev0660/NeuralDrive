Powerful Features Built for AI Sales Training

Give your reps realistic practice, instant coaching, and a smarter path to consistent performance.

📞 Realistic AI Sales Simulations

Train salespeople with ultra-realistic, voice-based outbound call simulations that mimic real dealership sales scenarios.

🔁 Scenario Customization

We can build and tweak AI customer personas and scenarios to fit any sales process, product line, or dealership campaign.

📊 Automatic Sales Rep Evaluation

Each call is analyzed instantly with AI-driven feedback, giving reps detailed scores and coaching on objection handling, tone, product knowledge, and more.

📈 Performance Analytics Dashboard

Managers get full visibility into rep performance with real-time dashboards, historical comparisons, and exportable reports. (Currently in the works)

🧠 AI Objection Library

Continuously expanding database of real-world objections like “I need to talk to my wife first” that AI customers can throw at sales reps, ensuring reps are prepared for common and difficult objections.

🕒 Call Scheduling & Reminders

Allow reps to schedule practice calls and receive reminders, creating consistent daily training habits without manual oversight. (Currently in the works)

🎯 Personalized Training Plans

Automatically generate improvement plans based on a rep’s performance history, such as focusing on price objections or improving call openers. (Currently in the works)

🎥 Post-Call Replay & Annotations

Reps and managers can review recordings with AI-generated annotations showing key moments, missed opportunities, and strengths. (Currently in the works)

🔄 Multi-Scenario Rotation

Rotate between different call scenarios like lease vs. finance, first-time buyers, trade-in discussions, and cold leads so reps don’t get too comfortable with one script.
